My name is Lianna Rose Weiksner and I am a graphic design student at the Cleveland Institute of Art. I love to work in Adobe programs and create things such as digital paintings, collages, and illustrations. My pieces range from being bright and colorful to dark and grungy. My work usually features a still subject or focal point and I really enjoy experimenting with different color schemes.
